# Product FAQ

## Account Recovery
To reset your password, go to Settings > Security > Reset Password. A verification code will be sent to your registered email. If you no longer have access to your email, contact support.

## Payment Methods
We accept Visa, Mastercard, and local Korean bank transfers (계좌이체). PayPal is supported for international orders.

## Order Cancellation
Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placement. After preparation begins, cancellation is not possible. Refunds for cancelled orders are processed within 3-5 business days.

## Customer Support Hours
Support is available Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM KST. Weekend support is available via email only.
