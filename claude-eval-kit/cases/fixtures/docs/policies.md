# Refund Policy

Orders may be refunded within 14 days of delivery. Damaged items are eligible for immediate replacement or full refund. Orders older than 30 days are not eligible for refund. Contact support@example.com for assistance.

# Delivery Areas

We deliver to all districts within Seoul, Incheon, and Gyeonggi Province. Orders outside these areas are not currently supported. Delivery takes 1-3 business days within Seoul and 3-5 days for other supported areas.

# Allergen Information

Bibimbap contains sesame, soy, and may contain traces of shellfish. All products are prepared in facilities that handle nuts, wheat, dairy, and eggs. Gluten-free options are labeled on the product page.
